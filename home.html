Authentic Hens

A hen is female chicken that is at least a year old. The hen is housed in a coop. As a result, the hen's shelter name is 'coop'. It is important to keep the hen in the chicken in order to protect them from predators and unfavorable environmental conditions.

Ready Chicken

Keep raw chicken and its juices away from ready-to-eat foods, like salads or food that is already cooked. Raw chicken is ready to cook and doesn't need to be washed first.

Frozen Chicken

Frozen chicken is simply a chicken food product available at the supermarket that can last for several months in a freezer. It is more prone to drying. On the other hand, a fresh chicken product has never been frozen and is made directly available in the market.

Parts Of Chicken
